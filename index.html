Welcome to SandBoxd Inc. business site.

We're a growing startup located in Canada, with a unique mindset and endless motivation to succeed.

Our games have had thousands of addicted players throughout the years. A family-like community surround us and actively participate on our community-driven cloud gaming platform: SandBoxd.

We believe that gaming should be cross platform and available from anywhere and on any device. Good games are played by the many, but great games have an active community playing and participating in them. Therefore, we built the SandBoxd gaming platform, allowing developers to host their games while surrounding them with our great community.

We are a team of young adventurists working hard for our ideas to become reality. If you believe in our mission and would like to join our journey, contact us.
